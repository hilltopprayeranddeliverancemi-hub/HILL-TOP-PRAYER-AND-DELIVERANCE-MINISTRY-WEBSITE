Welcome

Welcome to Hilltop Prayer and Deliverance Ministry, a place of prayer, worship, healing, deliverance, and spiritual transformation.

We are committed to proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurturing believers in faith, and helping people experience the power and presence of God.

Our Mission

  • To preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations.
  • To lead people into a deeper relationship with God.
  • To provide prayer, counseling, and deliverance ministry.
  • To raise disciples who live according to God's Word.
  • To transform lives through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

To be a ministry where lives are changed, souls are saved, families are restored, and God's presence brings healing, deliverance, hope, and spiritual growth.

About Us

Hilltop Prayer and Deliverance Ministry is dedicated to serving God and impacting lives through prayer, worship, biblical teaching, evangelism, discipleship, and community outreach.

What We Believe

  • The Holy Bible is the inspired Word of God.
  • Salvation is through Jesus Christ alone.
  • The power of prayer changes lives.
  • The Holy Spirit works in believers today.
  • Healing and deliverance are available through faith in Christ.
  • Jesus Christ is Lord and Savior.
